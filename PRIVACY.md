# Privacy Policy — Renfe Easy Booking

**Last updated:** 2026-03-22

## Data Collection

Renfe Easy Booking does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data used by the extension (booking preferences, session state) is stored locally in your browser's session storage and is automatically cleared when the browser is closed.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions, used exclusively for its core functionality:

- **Storage** — To persist booking state across page navigations during a single session.
- **Scripting** — To display error dialogs on the active tab.
- **ActiveTab** — To interact with the current Renfe tab.
- **Host permissions (renfe.com)** — To automate form filling on Renfe's website.

## Third-Party Services

This extension interacts only with renfe.com as directed by the user. No data is sent to any other third party.
